Privacy Policy

How your data is collected, used and protected

This Privacy Policy explains how ft_transcendence collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Service.

ft_transcendence is a non-commercial educational project developed by students as part of the School 42 curriculum. We aim to collect only what is necessary to operate the game.

1. Data We Collect

Depending on your use of the Service, we may collect:

  • Account data: username/nickname, email address (if required for login).
  • Game data: scores, progress, rankings, match history, in-game settings.
  • Technical data: basic server logs (e.g., IP address, timestamps) for security and troubleshooting.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (e.g., health, political opinions, biometric data).

2. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts.
  • To enable multiplayer gameplay (sessions, matchmaking, rankings).
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse (e.g., cheating, attacks).
  • To improve stability and performance during the educational development process.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing.

Data may be accessed by the student development team for maintenance and debugging purposes only. If we are legally required to disclose data by an official authority, we may comply as required by law.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored on servers used for the project and is accessible only to the project team.

We retain personal data only as long as needed to operate the Service during the project. At the end of the academic project, data may be deleted or anonymized.

5. Cookies

The Service may use technical session cookies that are strictly necessary for authentication and maintaining your session (e.g., keeping you logged in).

We do not use advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including the GDPR), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where consent is used as a legal basis (if applicable).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

7. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect data (access control, basic security practices). However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy during development. Updates are effective when posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact: aneumann+team@student.42.fr
